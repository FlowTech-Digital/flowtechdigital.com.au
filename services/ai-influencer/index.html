Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms can AI influencers post to?

We support all major platforms including Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, and X (Twitter). The Starter package includes 2 platforms, Pro includes 4.

How realistic are the AI-generated videos?

We use cutting-edge AI video generation technology. Videos feature natural lip-sync, expressions, and gestures. Quality continues to improve monthly.

Can I request specific content or topics?

Absolutely. You have full creative control. We work from your content calendar and can accommodate specific campaigns, product launches, or trending topics.

What if I want to change my AI influencer's look?

Minor refinements are included in all packages. Major persona changes (new look, voice, or personality) can be accommodated for an additional fee.

Do I own the AI influencer?

Yes. You own full rights to your AI persona, including all generated content. You can use the content however you wish.

What's the minimum commitment?

Starter package: 3 months. Pro package: 6 months. This ensures enough time to establish your AI influencer's presence and measure results.
