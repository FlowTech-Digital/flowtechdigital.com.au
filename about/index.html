About FlowTech Digital

FlowTech Digital is the web and branding division of FlowTech AI, a Sydney-based technology company. We design and build websites, brand identities and digital presence systems for Australian small businesses — particularly trades, home services and professional services.

How We Work

Every project follows the same process: we learn your business, design to a brief, build on a modern stack, and hand you a finished product you own outright. No subscriptions required, no lock-in, no ongoing fees unless you want them.

Sites are built on CloudFlare’s global network for speed and reliability. Branding is delivered as a complete system — logo, colour palette, typography, guidelines and file packs — so everything stays consistent as you grow.

What We Value

Quality First

Every site is custom-built to a professional standard. No cookie-cutter templates, no corners cut.

Honest Pricing

Fixed quotes, upfront. You know what you’re paying before work begins, and there are no surprise invoices.

Fast Turnaround

Most projects are delivered in one to two weeks, not months. We respect your time and your launch timeline.

Direct Communication

You deal with the person building your site, not an account manager. Questions get answered, not forwarded.

Part of Something Bigger

FlowTech Digital sits within the FlowTech AI ecosystem alongside divisions in trades software, digital fabrication and business automation. That means we draw on shared infrastructure, design systems and tooling that a standalone freelancer simply can’t match — while keeping the personal service of a small team.
